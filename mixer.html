MIT, Wellesley College, and Northeastern University are excited to host the Boston Intercollegiate Pre-health Student Mixer on March 8th, 2022 from 6-8 pm EST! Join fellow pre-health students across Boston for a night of informative panels tailored to your needs about preparing for the journey to medical school and socialize virtually with peers with similar interests!



Time & Date: March 28, 2022 6-8 pm EST
Location: Zoom (Link will be sent to students who register)
RSVP: https://tinyurl.com/BostonPrehealthMixer
Event schedule
PANELS
Join us to learn from pre-health about topics including:

  • Finding clinical experience in Boston (& virtually)
  • Picking medical school programs
  • What do do before med school (besides academics)
  • Places to do research in Boston
  • First-years: structuring the pre-med path - classes, extracurriculars, + more
  • Upperclassmen: Tips on Academics and the MCAT

    Ariel Ackermann
    MIT Prehealth & Career Advisor

    Ariel works with students and alums interested in applying to medical, dental, and other health-related professional schools. She also meets with students to develop and implement their educational and career plans. Ariel's background includes extensive international experience in higher education and social services at MIT and abroad, having lived in 6 countries over the course of ten years. She has a B.A. in Culture & Communication from Ithaca College and a M.A. in Intercultural Theology from Georg-August-Universität in Göttingen, Germany.

    Cindy Seltzer
    Wellesley College Helen Wallace '33, Director of Health Professions Advising

    Cindy advises students and alumnae as they prepare for careers in medicine and dentistry, as well as all other patient-facing health professions. Prior to working at Wellesley College, she served in similar roles at Northeastern University, where she worked with health professions and nursing students. She has a BA in Psychology from Kalamazoo College, a MA in Higher Education Administration from Boston College, and an MBA and a Doctorate of Education from Northeastern University. Since COVID-19 lockdowns began, Cindy has spent some of her free time learning to play the didgeridoo, training in parkour, and baking sourdough bread. She looks forward to seeing everyone at the mixer!

    Ciara O'Donnell
    Northeastern University Assistant Director of the PreMed and PreHealth Advising Program

She received her bachelor's degree in Sociology and master's degree in Guidance & Counseling at Providence College.
