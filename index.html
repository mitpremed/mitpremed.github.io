Peer Mentorship

MPS has a big/little matching program that pairs underclassmen pre-meds with upperclassmen in the field, so that they have a peer mentor that can provide guidance and advice for classes and extracurriculars.

Networking and Panels

In the past, we've hosted panels with physicians from reputable hospitals in the Boston area, as well as medical school admissions officers from schools such as Harvard and Tufts.

Community and Support

We know that preparing for medical school can be stressful and frustrating sometimes. MPS often sends out care packages, and we are planning on having bonding events as well to de-stress!
